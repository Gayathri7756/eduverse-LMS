## Test Flow

```
1. Login
   ↓
2. Go to My Learning
   ↓
3. Click "⚡ Quick Complete"
   ↓
4. Progress updates to 100%
   ↓
5. Click "🎓 Generate Certificate"
   ↓
6. View Certificate Page
   ↓
7. Click "📥 Download Certificate"
   ↓
8. Success! 🎉
```

## Color Scheme

- **Quick Complete Button**: Purple (#9333ea)
- **Certificate Button**: Green (#22c55e)
- **Progress Bar**: Blue to Indigo gradient
- **Certificate Background**: Light gradient (indigo-50 to blue-50)
- **Certificate Border**: Indigo (#4f46e5)

## Button States

### Quick Complete Button
- **Before**: Purple, clickable
- **After**: Disappears, replaced by Certificate button

### Certificate Button
- **Appears**: When all three = 100%
- **Color**: Green
- **Hover**: Darker green
- **Click**: Redirects to certificate page
