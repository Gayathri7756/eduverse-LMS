# 🎤 AI Voice Assistant - Quick Start Guide

## What's New?
Your Student Dashboard now has an **AI Voice Assistant** that can listen to your voice and respond with spoken audio!

## How to Use (3 Simple Steps)

### Step 1: Open Dashboard
- Go to `http://localhost:5174`
- Login with your student account
- Click "My Learning" in the navbar
- You'll see the **AI Voice Assistant** at the top

### Step 2: Start Speaking
- Click the **"Start Listening"** button
- Speak clearly into your microphone
- Examples:
  - "What should I study today?"
  - "Help me with machine learning"
  - "Create a study plan"
  - "What are best practices for Python?"

### Step 3: Listen to Response
- The AI will respond with spoken audio
- Your conversation appears in the chat
- Ask follow-up questions anytime
- Click "Clear Chat" to start over

## Features

✅ **Speech Recognition** - Understands your voice commands
✅ **AI Responses** - Intelligent answers from Gemini API
✅ **Text-to-Speech** - Hears responses spoken aloud
✅ **Chat History** - See all your conversations
✅ **Easy Controls** - Simple buttons to control everything

## Control Buttons

| Button | Function |
|--------|----------|
| 🎤 Start Listening | Begin voice recognition |
| ⏹️ Stop Listening | End voice recognition |
| 🔇 Stop Speaking | Interrupt AI speech |
| ✕ Clear Chat | Reset conversation |

## Status Indicators

- 🔴 **Red pulse** = Listening to you
- 🟢 **Green pulse** = Speaking response
- 📝 **Text box** = Shows what you said

## Troubleshooting

### "Microphone not working?"
1. Check browser permissions (click 🔒 in address bar)
2. Allow microphone access
3. Refresh the page
4. Try again

### "Can't hear the response?"
1. Check your speaker volume
2. Check browser volume (not muted)
3. Try a different browser
4. Restart your browser

### "Speech not recognized?"
1. Speak clearly and slowly
2. Reduce background noise
3. Move closer to microphone
4. Try again

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Speak Clearly** - Enunciate words properly
2. **Reduce Noise** - Find a quiet environment
3. **Use Headphones** - Better audio quality
4. **Ask Specific Questions** - More detailed questions get better answers
5. **Be Patient** - Wait for AI to finish speaking before asking next question
