# How to Get Certificate - Step by Step

## Current Status
- Progress shows 0% because you haven't completed anything yet
- Certificate button will appear when ALL three are 100%

## Complete Course to Get Certificate

### Step 1: Watch All 5 Videos
1. Go to "My Learning"
2. Click "Continue" on "React for Beginners"
3. Click on "Videos" tab
4. Click on each video (1-5):
   - 1. Introduction & Fundamentals
   - 2. Core Concepts & Basics
   - 3. Advanced Techniques
   - 4. Best Practices & Patterns
   - 5. Real-World Projects
5. **Result**: Videos progress = 100%

### Step 2: Pass All 5 Quizzes
1. Click on "Quizzes" tab
2. For each quiz (1-5):
   - Click "Start Quiz"
   - Answer all questions
   - Click "Submit Quiz"
   - See results (✓ or ✕)
3. **Result**: Quizzes progress = 100%

### Step 3: Submit All 5 Assignments
1. Click on "Assignments" tab
2. For each assignment (1-5):
   - Click "Start Assignment"
   - Enter submission text
   - Click "Submit Assignment"
   - See "✓ Submitted" button
3. **Result**: Assignments progress = 100%

### Step 4: Certificate Appears
1. Go back to "My Learning"
2. **Expected**: See progress breakdown:
   - Videos: 100%
   - Quizzes: 100%
   - Assignments: 100%
3. **Expected**: Green "🎓 Generate Certificate" button appears
4. Click button → Certificate generated!

## Troubleshooting

### Certificate Button Not Showing
**Check**:
- Videos: 100%? (all 5 watched)
- Quizzes: 100%? (all 5 passed)
- Assignments: 100%? (all 5 submitted)

**If not all 100%**:
- Complete remaining items
- Refresh page
- Button should appear

### Progress Not Updating
**Try**:
- Refresh page
- Check that items are marked complete
- Look for checkmarks (✓)

### Can't See Progress Breakdown
**Check**:
- Are you in My Learning?
- Is course card showing?
- Look for three boxes: Videos, Quizzes, Assignments
